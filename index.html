Weize Liu

PhD Student @ UMD

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park, advised by Prof. Furong Huang.

My research interests lie in Large Language Models (LLMs) and Vision-Language Models (VLMs), with a particular focus on synthetic data, reasoning, and agentic capabilities. Earlier projects centered on improving the reasoning depth and breadth of LLMs through principled data generation and distillation. I am currently exploring how to endow LLMs with stronger agent behaviors.
